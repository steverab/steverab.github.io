I am Stephan, a Computer Science Master's student at Technische Universität München (TUM). In addition to my main studies, I am also studying Technology Management at the Center for Digital Technology and Management (CDTM), an interdisciplinary honours study program.

During my current studies, I am specializing in a sub-area of Artificial Intelligence called Machine Learning. Right now, I am a Visiting Research Scholar at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) working on my Master's thesis together with Prof. Zachary Lipton. As part of this work, we are evaulating current and developing new methods for detecting and quantifying shifts in the data distribution between training and testing environments.

Aside from technology, science, software development, and entrepreneurship, I am also interested in UI/UX design, architecture, and aviation.
